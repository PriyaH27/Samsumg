📌 About This Project

This comprehensive exploratory data analysis examines Dubai's real estate market, identifying trends and patterns in property transactions, pricing, and market dynamics.

🎯 Key Objectives

Data Exploration

Identify areas with highest/lowest average property prices

Trend Analysis

Track property transaction changes over time

Price Patterns

Analyze residential property price trends and seasonal patterns

Market Insights

Understand property type distribution in 2023

📚 Key Findings

  • Island 2 has the highest median price for residential properties
  • Al Lusaily has the lowest median price for residential properties
  • Significant transaction increases in 2008 and 2020
  • Yearly median prices showed significant growth starting from 2001
  • Highest median prices occur in the middle of the year (seasonal pattern)
  • Residential properties account for 92.1% of total 2023 transactions
  • Unit-type properties comprise 83% of residential transactions

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

  • Data Source: Dubai Land Department (Dubai Pulse)
  • Analysis: Python, Jupyter Notebook, Kaggle
  • Visualization: Tableau Public (Interactive Dashboard)
  • Report: Google Sites (Static Analysis)
